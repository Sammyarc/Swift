Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We are here to help

Are my personal information safe with Swift?

Swift employs state-of-the-art security measures, including encryption and secure protocols to safeguard your financial data.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account with Swift is a breeze! Simply visit our website and click on the "Sign Up" button. Follow the easy step-by-step registration process, providing the required information. Once completed, you'll have your Swift account ready to explore and manage your financial journey.

How quickly can I access a loan?

Swift offers a streamlined and efficient loan application process. Once you submit your application, our team works diligently to provide a swift response. The time to access a loan may vary based on factors like application completeness and verification. Rest assured, we strive to make the process as quick and convenient as possible.

Are my savings goals visible to others?

Your financial goals are entirely private and visible only to you. Swift prioritizes the confidentiality of your savings objectives. Your personal dashboard is secure, and information about your savings goals is kept confidential. You have complete control over who can access your financial information.

What If I miss a loan repayment?

Life can be unpredictable, and we understand that circumstances may arise. If you find yourself unable to make a loan repayment, it's crucial to communicate with us promptly. Contact our customer support, and we'll work together to explore possible solutions, such as adjusting your repayment schedule. We're here to support you through financial challenges.
